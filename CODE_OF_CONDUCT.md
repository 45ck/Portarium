# Code Of Conduct

Portarium is an open-source project for people building safer AI-agent systems.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep technical disagreement focused on facts, trade-offs, and evidence.
- Help newcomers understand the project before asking them to absorb internal history.
- Prefer clear issues, reproducible examples, and testable proposals.
- Respect security boundaries and responsible disclosure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Publicly disclosing security vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a chance to respond.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private maintainer discussion where possible or contact the project maintainers through GitHub.

For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, hide, or remove comments and may limit participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
